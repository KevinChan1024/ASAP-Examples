!! Description: A simple parabolic mirror using
!! the LENSES command.

SYSTEM NEW
RESET

LENSES
  MIRROR Z 0  1  -2
  
WINDOW Y Z
WINDOW 1.5  !! Enlarge 50%
PLOT LENS 'LENS:MIRROR' COLORS 11
RETURN
